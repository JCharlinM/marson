# Arte Madferro - Roberto Marson
## Website Design Notes

### Artist Profile
- **Name:** Roberto Marson
- **Medium:** Wood (reclaimed/salvaged quality pieces) + Iron/Steel
- **Style:** Ancient/rupestrian aesthetic, giving new life to exceptional aged wood
- **Background:** Retired history professor, philosophy lover, heavy metal enthusiast (Metallica, etc.)
- **Unique angle:** Blend of intellectual depth (history/philosophy) with raw metal aesthetic

### Materials Observed
- Aged wood with incredible texture and grain
- Black iron/steel bands and accents
- Edison bulb lighting integrated into pieces
- Functional art (lamps, decorative objects)

### Visual System Concept

**Color Palette:**
- Aged wood tones: warm amber (#D4A574), honey gold (#C8945F)
- Iron/steel: deep charcoal (#2B2B2B), oxidized black (#1A1A1A)
- Earth tones: burnt sienna, raw umber
- Background: warm off-white (#F5F2ED) or deep charcoal for contrast
- Accent: oxidized copper/bronze for CTAs

**Typography:**
- Heading: Strong, bold serif (Playfair Display, Cinzel) - classical sophistication
- Body: Clean, readable sans (Montserrat, Work Sans) - modern clarity
- Accent: Maybe a distressed/industrial font for gallery titles

**Layout Philosophy:**
- Grid gallery: let each piece breathe, generous whitespace
- Detailed views: large imagery, zoom capability
- Texture overlays: subtle grain/metal textures
- Contrast: light backgrounds to showcase dark wood/metal pieces

**Trilingual Support:**
- PT-BR (primary)
- Español
- English
- Language switcher in header

### Site Structure
1. **Hero/Landing** - Featured piece, artist statement
2. **Galería/Portfolio** - Grid of works with filtering
3. **Biografía** - Roberto's story (history, philosophy, metalero identity)
4. **Contacto** - Contact form, social, location if relevant

### Variations to Explore
1. **Classic Gallery** - White background, minimal, sophisticated
2. **Industrial Dark** - Charcoal backgrounds, metal textures, edgy
3. **Warm Artisan** - Earth tones, textured backgrounds, craft-focused
4. **Bold Contrast** - High contrast B&W with wood tone accents
